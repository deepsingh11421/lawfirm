Advisory

A S Partners philosophy is to endeavor for a practical, useful route to achieving the goals of our clients in the quickest and most economical manner possible. If you are someone who is seeking advice in the following services, you can contact us:

The law in this area is complex and challenging so it is essential that you have the support of a dynamic and experienced legal team. A S Partners is regarded as having one of the leading Corporate & Commercial practices in India.
As we know Start-ups face battles starting from idea, to hiring, to financing, to distinguishing themselves from the vast crowds of competitors fighting for market share and publicity and not to mention the statutory legal and tax compliances which a start-up needs to fulfill. Advice is rendered on a broad spectrum, ranging from general corporate advice, corporate governance, contract law and exchange control regulations to mergers and acquisition, takeover code issues and outbound investments.
We make it our priority to really understand your business needs and objectives to enable us to provide commercially focused, practical guidance specifically tailored to your individual circumstances and business interests. Due to our holistic approach, we have successfully dealt with many complex transactions and have developed long-standing relationships with our clients.
The following services are provided by us to facilitate new/existing startups:
• Incorporation of Company
• Registration and Recognition as Start-up with Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion
• Drafting and vetting of contracts of commercial nature
• Awareness and Adoption of the Intellectual Property Rights
• Facilitating Start-ups in Manufacturing Sector
• Winding Up and Exit Strategies for Start-ups
• Litigation

Besides legal consulting and litigation services . We provide various other legal services including Documentation, execution and their registration with the concerned authority. We assist our clients in conducting due diligence for assessing legal position and credibility of their potential business partners, before entering into major transactions like joint ventures, mergers, acquisitions, transfer of shares, commercial lease, etc. We conduct due diligence of corporate documents, title deeds and records, capital and share holding details, credits and borrowing, tax, valuation of assets and intellectual property, filings with the ROC, etc. Our due diligence reports are comprehensive, integrated and present true valuations. Our due diligence meets the specific requirements of the industry and the transaction. A S Partners Due Diligence team can help you with:
• Conducting comprehensive Legal Due Diligence
