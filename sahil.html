BIOGRAPHY

Mr. Sahil Mahajan: He is an energetic and vigorous advocate with an experience of about 5 years in the field of law. His legal career initiated under the guidance of 'Mr. Narender Chauhan' who is known for his excellence in Criminal Law thereafter he had joined 'Areness law', a law firm situated in Punjabi Bagh, Delhi. His experience comprises of all spheres of law and has hands on experience in handling matters relating to constitutional, civil and criminal laws. He specializes particularly with Criminal law. He has acquired diverse experience in handling matters relating to criminal and quasi criminal enactments. He is betrothed with the responsibility to look after the cases of the firm at District Court level and has been working brilliantly for the rapid growth of our firm.
